Online Help:

The Airport Finder application helps citizens locate an airport and obtain information about aviation facilities in their community. This application is intended to used by Massachusetts residents, but can be used by visitors to locate an airport near them, or one that offers the activity they are interested in.

To locate an airport, simply enter an address, activity, or airport name in the search box or select activities. Or, you can use your current location to find an airport near you. The airport area(s) will then be highlighted on the map and relevant information about available aviation facilities presented to you.

Using the Application

The Airport Finder application provides residents and visitors an easy way to find airports near them. Enter your address or click on the Current Location button to find airports near you. If you click the Current Location button, it will use the location of your computer's IP address or your cell phone/tablet location to find airports near you. When you enter an address or use your current location, the application will present a list of airports in the vicinity of your search and allow you to review relevant information about each airport. In addition, you can search for an airport by name, or specific service you'd like to be available. To do that, enter the name of the airport in the search box. When you enter the name of the airport, the application will find the airport or present a list of airports that offer the recreation activity you're interested in and allow you to review relevant information about each airport.

Finding an Area of Interest

There are three primary ways to identify an area of interest. You can interact directly with the map and click on an airport when you've located it. You can also use your current location or an address to find an airport near you. Finally, you can search for an airport by name.

Map Based Navigation

There are several ways to interact with the map:

1. Use the basemap switcher to select the basemap you'd like to interact with
2. Hold down the shift key and drag a box on the map to zoom in
3. Hold Down Ctrl+Shift to zoom out
4. Roll your mouse roller to zoom in/out
5. Click the + or - to zoom in/out

Searching

The search box can be used to find an address or airport and recreation area you're interested in:

1. Enter the address or airport name you're interested in
2. Click on the item in the list to locate the address or click on the airport in the list on the left panel

You can enter a partial address and then identify the correct value in the list.

The Activities panel can be used to search for airports by recreational activities:

1. Click on each activity you are interested in and click search.

aviation facilities

When you find an airport you're interested in, you can select it from the list and it will then be highlighted on the map. Additional detail about each airport and the recreational activities available will be provided in the information panel. When you click on an individual airport on the map, the same information will be displayed.

Directions

You can also get directions to each airport using your current location or an address you select. When you find an airport you'd like to visit, enter an address to start from, or click the use current location button, to calculate the directions and route.

Share Your Map

You can click on the Share Map button to share your map several different ways. You can compose an email, post to your Facebook wall or post a Tweet. When you use the Facebook or Twitter links, you'll be asked to sign in to your respective account.

More Information

The Airport Finder application can used by Local Government organizations responsible for managing airports and aviation facilities to deploy a web-based airport locator application. This application provides 24x7 access to the organization and typically supplements customer service phone numbers staffed by these organizations.

Please visit Esri's ArcGIS for Local Government Resource Center for additional information.

Browser Compatibility

The Airport Finder is supported on the following browsers: Microsoft IE 7/8/9/10, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome and Apple Safari.
